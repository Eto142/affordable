What is Forex trading?

Foreign exchange, or forex for short, involves trading one currency pair against another. Traders predict the direction of one currency against another in pairs like Euro vs US Dollar (EUR/USD).

Why trade Forex?

The Forex market is the world’s most liquid market. It provides traders with the opportunity to trade around the clock, 24 hours per day, 5 days a week. This allows traders to take advantage of an unprecedented number of opportunities, compared to conventional markets that close at the end of each day.
